# Benchmark Results — 2026-05-24

Node.js v24 / Windows 11 / 2 000 averaged runs after 200 warmup

## Renderer update() — ms per frame

| Renderer                       | 1 000 particles | 10 000 particles |
|-------------------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| BillboardRenderer              | 0.0070 ms       | 0.0898 ms        |
| LineRenderer                   | 0.0080 ms       | 0.0965 ms        |
| InstanceRenderer               | 0.0806 ms       | 0.7127 ms        |
| CollectionRenderer (×2 meshes) | 0.0719 ms       | 0.7200 ms        |

All renderers comfortably under the 16 ms frame budget at 10 000 particles.
InstanceRenderer and CollectionRenderer are matrix-write dominated (setMatrixAt × N).
