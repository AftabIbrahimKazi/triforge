## Notes

`build()` is a one-shot geometry construction call, not a per-frame operation. In typical
usage strands are generated once and the geometry is built once; per-frame animation drives
material uniforms (wind, color ramp) rather than rebuilding geometry. The 16 ms budget
applies to per-frame work; build is exempt from this constraint.

For interactive groom tools that need live rebuild, cap at 500 tube strands (~11 ms) or
2000 ribbon strands (~11 ms).
